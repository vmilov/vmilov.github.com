Chess Instruction

Chess is a hard game combining sport, science and art. Improving at chess requires hard work, perseverance and creativity. Most players can reach master level with hard work and perseverance. I can guide students well but one still needs to put in a lot of effort. I am thus looking for serious students. I usually start with a student's tournament games. It is important to study those games and identify typical mistakes. After identifying mistake patterns, it's time to decide what material to review, problems to solve, and books to study.
